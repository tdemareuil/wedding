About Me

Hello, I'm Thomas de Mareuil.

My name is Thomas de Mareuil, I'm 24 and I will soon graduate from Ecole Polytechnique's MSc Data Science. Before diving into the world of data, I studied Management & Economics at HEC Paris.

I would love to use data science to solve exciting challenges, and to work with tech-oriented companies with a positive impact. Here are some of the projects I've been exploring.

  • Machine Learning

    Using Python and R to get valuable insights from data and build predictive models.

  • Cloud Computing

    Using Spark, Azure and other frameworks to leverage cloud resources.

  • Deep Learning

    Using Keras, Tensorflow, Pytorch to build neural networks and analyse text & images.

  • Web Apps

    Packaging models into useable tools to solve business problems.

  • Videos

    I also love travelling and editing videos of my trips with friends.

  • Other hobbies

    Piano, guitar, theatre, skiing... Always looking for new things to learn!
